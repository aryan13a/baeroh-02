Case Study 01

The Jaipur Workspace

A complete office fit-out designed to challenge standard workplace sterile environments. The concept focuses on warmth, raw textures, and silent acoustic transitions. Oak partitions and curved dark plaster cladding create workspaces that foster deep focus and unhurried collaboration.

Client: Private Workspace Group Location: Jaipur, India Completed: 2026
01 / Collaboration Lounge

The central meeting space blends textured linen curtains with robust oak tables and sand-toned seating, keeping the atmosphere calm and residential.

02 / Focus Meeting Area

Dark charcoal-stained wood contrast beautifully against the ivory walls, creating a defined frame for private discussions.

03 / The Meeting Pod

A sculptural curved wall cladded in deep dark plaster provides high acoustic insulation, hosting the main pod entryway.

04 / The Workstation Row

Generous oak desks feature built-in acoustic screens wrapped in textured fabric, providing clarity and personal focus in a shared layout.

05 / The Mirror Alcove

A quiet recess with custom plaster styling, soft brass fittings, and a curved mirror creating a moment of calm.

06 / Curation and styling detail

Earthy ceramics and a minimalist tripod mantel clock rest upon a bespoke oak credenza, embodying silent detail.
